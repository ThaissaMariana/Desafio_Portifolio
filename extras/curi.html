Barbie and Shrek Trivia

Trivia about Barbie

  • Barbie was created in 1959 by Mattel, and her first version was an adult-looking doll.
  • Barbie's full name is Barbara Millicent Roberts.
  • Barbie has had over 200 different careers, including astronaut, doctor, and president of the United States.
  • In 2019, Barbie got a new look, with different body types, skin tones, and hairstyles.
  • Barbie has had more than 60 million different outfits created over the years.

Trivia about Shrek

  • The character Shrek is based on the children's book by William Steig, released in 1990.
  • Shrek was voiced by Mike Myers, who originally gave the character a Scottish accent.
  • The first Shrek movie, released in 2001, was a huge success and won the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature.
  • Shrek's face was inspired by a monster from "Gumby," a famous animated character from the 1950s.
  • The Shrek movie series is one of the highest-grossing animation franchises in history, with over 3 billion dollars in box office revenue.
